📚 Financial Resources

Types of Loans

  • Personal Loan - Unsecured loan for various purposes
  • Home Loan - For purchasing or constructing a house
  • Car Loan - Specifically for vehicle purchase
  • Education Loan - For funding education expenses
  • Gold Loan - Secured loan against gold ornaments
  • Business Loan - For business expansion or working capital

Tips for Better Loan Approval

  • Maintain a good credit score (750+)
  • Keep your debt-to-income ratio below 40%
  • Provide accurate documentation
  • Consider adding a co-applicant with good credit
  • Compare offers from multiple lenders

Financial Glossary

  • EMI - Equated Monthly Installment: Fixed payment amount made by a borrower to a lender at a specified date each calendar month
  • APR - Annual Percentage Rate: The annual rate charged for borrowing or earned through an investment
  • Collateral - An asset that a borrower offers as a way for a lender to secure the loan
  • Credit Score - A number between 300-850 that depicts a consumer's creditworthiness
